CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Cale J. Bradford
2nd District
Cale J. Bradford was appointed to the Court on August 1, 2007.
He received a B.A. in labor relations and personnel management
from Indiana University Bloomington in 1982 and his J.D. from
Indiana University School of Law-Indianapolis in 1986. Judge
Bradford served for more than 10 years as Judge of the Marion
Superior Court and was twice elected presiding judge. He served
as Chief Trial Deputy in the Marion County Prosecutor's Office for
two years, and for five years was an Assistant United States
Attorney for the Southern District of Indiana. He engaged in the
private practice of law from 1986 to 1991. He was a member of the
Indiana Judges Criminal Policy Committee and the Board of
Directors of the Indiana State Judicial Conference. He is a
Distinguished Fellow of the Indianapolis Bar Association and has
taught ICLEF seminars on trial practice for more than 10 years.

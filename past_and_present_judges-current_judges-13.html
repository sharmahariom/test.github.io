CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Edward W. Najam, Jr.
1st District
Edward W. Najam, Jr was appointed to the Court in December of
1992. Judge Najam was raised in Bloomington and graduated from
Indiana University Bloomington (B.A, with Highest Distinction,
1969), where he was admitted to Phi Beta Kappa and elected
Student Body President (J.D., 1972). Judge Najam was Administrative
Assistant to the Mayor of Bloomington(1972-1974) and an attorney
in private practice (1974-1992). He has served as a member of the
Supreme Court Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure
(1995-2005) and the Supreme Court Judicial Technology and
Automation Committee (1999-2005). He was Chair of the Appellate
Practice Section of the Indiana State Bar Association (1997-1998).
Judge Najam is a graduate of the Indiana Graduate Program for
Judges. He is the Judicial Liaison to the Indiana Counter-
Terrorism and Security Council. Judge Najam is a member of the
Board of Visitors of the IU School of Law-Bloomington and is an
Eagle Scout.

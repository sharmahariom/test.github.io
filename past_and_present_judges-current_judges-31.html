CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Micheal P. Barnes
3rd District
Michael P. Barnes was appointed to the Court on May 22, 2000.
Judge Barnes received his B.A. from St. Ambrose College in
1970 and his J.D from the University of Notre Dame Law
School in 1973. He was a Deputy Prosecuting Attorney and
privately practiced law in South Bend from 1973-1978. In 1978,
he was elected the St. Joseph County Prosecuting Attorney, a
position he held for 20 years. During that tenure, Judge Barnes
was elected President of the National District Attorneys Association
(95-96), Chairman of the Board of the Indiana Prosecuting Attorneys
Council (92-93), National Board of Trial Advocacy (95-96), National
Advisory Council on Violence Against Women (97), Chairman
of the Board of Regents, National College of District Attorneys
(97-98), American Prosecutor's Research Institute (97-98),
and various other civic and professional organizations.

CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Elaine B. Brown
5th District
Elaine B. Brown was appointed to the Court in May 2008. She
is a native of Ferdinand and the mother of two adult children.
Judge Brown was a trial court judge for 15 years and before
that practiced law for 11 years in Jasper and Evansville. She
graduated with distinction from Indiana University-Bloomington
in 1976 and taught for three years in Jasper schools before
entering and earning her J.D. from Indiana University Law School-
Bloomington in 1982.She created Dubois County's Drug Court and
formed the county's first Community Corrections Advisory Board.
Judge Brown is an Indiana Judicial College graduate, served on
the Board of Directors of the Indiana Judicial Conference, Board
of Managers of the Indiana Judges Association, Supreme Court
Judges and Lawyers Assistance Program Committee, Board
of Governors of the Lugar Excellence in Public Service Series,
as a leader in the Brooks Inn of Court, and was a civil mediator.

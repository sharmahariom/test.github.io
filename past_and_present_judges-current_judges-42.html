CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Melissa S. May
4th District
Melissa S. May was appointed to the Court in April 1998, having
practiced law in Evansville for 14 years. Judge May graduated from
Indiana University South Bend with a B.S. in 1980 and from Indiana
University School of Law-Indianapolis in 1984. Judge May served on
the Indiana Bar Association Board of Managers from 1992-1994,
and as counsel to the President 2000-2001. In 2003, she was named
to the American Bar Association's Standing Committee on Attorney
Specialization and currently serves as its special counsel.
In 2004, she became an adjunct faculty member at Indiana University
School of Law-Indianapolis. Also in 2004, Judge May received an
Honorary Doctor of Civil Law degree from the University of Southern
Indiana. In 2008, Judge May was named Chair of the Indiana Pro
Bono Commission. From 1999 to 2004, she was a member
of Indiana's Continuing Legal Education Commission.

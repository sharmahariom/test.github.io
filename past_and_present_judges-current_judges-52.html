CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Margret G. Robb
5th District
Margret G. Robb was appointed to the Court in July 1998. She
graduated from Purdue (B.S., 1970, M.S., 1972) and Indiana
University School of Law-Indianapolis (J.D. magna cum laude,
1978). She practiced law in Lafayette, was a Bankruptcy
Trustee, a member of the Indiana Board of Law Examiners, and
was founding Chair of Governor Bowen's Commission on the
Status of Women. She chairs several Indiana Supreme Court
Task Forces and serves on the ABA Law School Accreditation
Committee. In numerous capacities, Judge Robb serves in numerous
capacities on the Indiana State, Indianapolis, Tippecanoe, and
American Bar associations, the Fellows of the Indiana State and
Indianapolis Bar foundations and the National Association of
Women Judges. She received the 1993 Indiana State Bar
Association's 'Celebrating 100 years of Women in the Legal
Profession' Award and its 2005 Women in the Law Recognition
Award, and the 2004 Ball State University Women of Achievement Award.

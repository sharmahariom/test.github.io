WEBCASTING ORAL ARGUMENTS

In January 2009 the Court of Appeals of Indiana
began webcasting most oral arguments held in the
Court of Appeals courtroom at the Indiana
Statehouse. Additionally, the oral arguments are
televised on-site on a monitor outside of the
courtroom.

The Court has a long history of outreach to local
communities through its traveling oral argument
schedule in an effort to show communities the oral
argument process. Webcasting oral arguments is
another way the court is reaching out to local
communities.

The webcasts are posted on our website.
Webcasting is now a standard process unless a
majortiy of 3-judge panel determines not
to do so.

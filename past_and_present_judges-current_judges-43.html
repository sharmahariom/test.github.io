CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Patricia A. Riley
4th District
Patricia A. Riley was appointed to the Court on January 1, 1994.
Judge Riley is a fifth-generation Hoossier and grew up in
Rensselaer. She graduated fron Indiana University in 1971 and
from Indiana University School of Law-Indianapolis in 1974. She
was a deputy prosecuting attorney for three years and a public
defender for one year in Marion County. In 1980, she moved
back to Rensselaer where she practiced law for 10 years. During
that time, she also taught at St. Joseph's College as adjunct
faculty. In July 1990, she was appointed Judge of Jasper
Superior Court #2. Judge Riley has served as a member of the
Boards of the National Association of Women Judges, Chair of
the Joint Committee of the Bench and Bar on Gender Issues in
the Profession, and Chair of the Appellate Practice Section
of the ISBA. Judge Riley also serves as adjunct faculty at the Indiana
University School of Law-Indianapolis.

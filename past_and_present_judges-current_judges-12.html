CURRENT JUDGES

Judge John G. Baker
1st District
John G. Baker was appointed to the Court on June 2, 1989.
Judge Baker was raised in Aurora in Dearborn County. He
graduated from Culver Military Academy in 1964, Indiana
University Bloomington in 1968, and received his J.D from
IU School of Law Bloomington in 1971. Judge Baker received
an LL.M. in judicial process from the University of Virginia
School of Law in 1995. In 1976, Judge Baker was appointed
to the Monroe County Court, then took the Monroe Superior
Court bench in 1977. He has served on the Board of Managers
of the Indiana Judges Association since 1980 and was president
from 1987-1989. In 2013, Judge Baker retired after 33 years
of teaching at IU Bloomington's School of Public and Environmental
Affairs. He continues to teach the American Legal System for
Foreign Lawyers at IU McKinney Law School.

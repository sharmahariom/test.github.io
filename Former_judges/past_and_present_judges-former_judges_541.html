FORMER JUDGES

Judge Ezra H. Friedlander
Ezra H. Friedlander was appointed to the Court on January 7, 1993.
He graduated from Indiana University Bloomington in 1962 and its
law School in 1995. Judge Friedlander was in private practice in Lake
County for three years and then moved to Indianapolis where he
served as corporation counsel for the Indiana Secretary of State in
1971. He maintained a private practice until his appointment to the
Court. He also served as a part-time deputy prosecutor in both Lake
and Marion counties. Judge Friedlander has served as co-chair of
The Indiana Supreme Court Commission on Race and Gender
Fairness. He also has served president for the Indiana University
Law School Alumni Association. Judge Friedlander is a member
of the Indiana University Law Alumni Fellows and serves on the
Board of Directors of the Indiana University Foundation.

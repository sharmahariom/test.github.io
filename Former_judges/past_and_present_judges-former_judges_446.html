FORMER JUDGES

Judge Carr L. Darden
Carr L. Darden was appointed to the Court in October 1994 and took
office in November of 1994. Judge Darden graduated from Indiana
University Kelly School of Business-Bloomington in 1966, and from
Indiana University School of Law-Indianapolis in 1970. He maintained
a general practice of Law for several years in Indianapolis. Judge
Darden also served as Chief Deputy State Public Defender upon the
passing of Public Defender Harriette Bailey Conn in 1981. In 1988, he
became a Master Commissioner in Marion County Superior Court,
Criminal Division Six. In June 1989, he was appointed to serve as a
Judge in the Marion County Municipal Court, and in 1991, he was
appointed to serve as a judge in the Marion County Superior Court.
Judge Darden is a 1998 graduate of the Indiana Judicial College. He
is also a June 2004 graduate of the Indiana Graduate program
for Judges.

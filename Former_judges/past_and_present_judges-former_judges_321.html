FORMER JUDGES

Judge Ira Batman
Judge Name: Ira Batman Ira Batman was born in Lawrence County, Indiana on January 20, 1862. He taught
school before attending Indiana University, graduating in 1885. He began practicing
law in Bloomington with Judge H.C. Duncan, then later with Robert Miller and
James Blair ion the firm of Batman, Miller & Blair. He married Mary Waldron in 1886,
and together they had a daughter.

He was city attorney for Bloomington for 10 years and prosecuting attorney for
Monroe and Owen counties for two years. In 19094, he was elected to the Indiana
House of Representatives.

In 1916, judge Batman was elected as a Republican to the Indiana Appellate Court
where he served two terms, from January 1, 1917 to January 1, 1925.
Judge Batman was a member for the Board of Trustees of Indiana university for 25
years. He ws also a member of the Benevolent and Protective Order of Elks, the
Columbia Club in Indianapolis, and the first Christian Church in Bloomington.
He died of a heart attack at home in Bloomington on April 10, 1934. Because of

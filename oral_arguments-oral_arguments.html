ORAL ARGUMENTS

The Court of Appeals of Indiana may, in its own
discretion, set oral argument on its own or a party's
motion. App.R. 52(A). Most oral arguments are
heard in the Court of Appeals courtroom on the
4th floor of the Indiana Statehouse; however a few
are heard in the Supreme Court courtroom on the
3rd floor of the Statehouse. Also, each year the
court hears oral argument at sites across the state.

The Court of Appeals of Indiana holds oral argument
in more than 35% of the cases in which oral
argument is requested. The court's oral argument
calendar is available on the court's website.
The calendar contains short synopses of the cases.

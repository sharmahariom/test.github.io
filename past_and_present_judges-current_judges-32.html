CURRENT JUDGES

Judge Terry A. Crone
3rd District
Terry A. Crone was appointed to the Court on March 8, 2004. Judge
Crone was raised in South Bend and graduated from DePauw
University, cum laude, in 1974 and from the University of Notre
Dame Law School in 1977. Judge Crone practiced Law in South
Bend for nine years and served as the St. Joseph County Attorney
from 1981 to 1986. In 1986. Judge Crone was appointed Magistrate
of the St.Joseph Circuit Court where he served until his
appointment as Judge of the St. Joseph Circuit Court in 1989.
Judge Crone is a past President of the St. Joseph County Bar
Association, a former Member of the Board of Managers of the
Indiana Judges Association, a former Member of the Supreme Court
Committee on Character and Fitness, and the Alternative Dispute
Resolution Committee of the Indiana Judicial Conference.

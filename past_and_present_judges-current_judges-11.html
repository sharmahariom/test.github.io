CURRENT JUDGES

Judge L . Mark Bailey
1st District
L. Mark Bailey was appointed to the Court in 1998. Judge Bailey
was raised on the Decatur County farm homesteaded by his
ancestors over 150 years ago. He earned his B.A from the
University of Indianapolis (1978); J.D from the Indiana University
School of Law-indianapolis (1982); and M.B.A. from Indiana
Wesleyan University (1999). Previously, Judge Bailey served as
Judge of the Decatur County and Decatur Superior Courts. He is
past chair of the Indiana Pro Bono Commission and received the
Pro Bono Publico and Randall Shepard Awards for pro bono
contributions. He was a Distinguised Jurist in Residence at
Stetson University College of Law and is currently an adjunct
professor at the University of Indianapolis. Judge Bailey serves on
the Supreme Court Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure,
the Judicial Education Committee of the Judicial Conference of
Indiana, and the board of Managers of the Indiana
Judges Association.
